Common questions

Quick answers to the things we hear most often.

How do I restore my progress?

Your garden and habit history are saved to your account in the cloud. Simply sign in with the same username on your new device and everything will be restored automatically. If you're having trouble, email us with your account username and we'll look into it.

I found a bug.

Sorry about that! Please describe what happened, what you expected, and the steps to reproduce it, then send it to rooted.app.help@gmail.com. Screenshots or a short screen recording are always helpful. We take every report seriously and aim to push fixes quickly.

I have a feature suggestion.

We'd love to hear it! Drop us an email with your idea and why it would be useful for your habit practice. We read every suggestion and many features in Rooted came from user feedback.

How do streaks and plant growth work?

Each habit tracks a current streak based on how consistently you log it. Your plant grows through four stages — Seedling, Sprout, Growing, and Blooming — as your streak increases. Missing a day will slow growth, but your plant won't disappear. Consistency is rewarded, not perfection.

How do I delete my account or data?

You can request full account and data deletion by emailing us at rooted.app.help@gmail.com. We will process the request promptly.
