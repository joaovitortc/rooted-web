Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025  ·  Effective immediately

Introduction

Welcome to Rooted. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Rooted app, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep things simple because we only collect what's truly necessary for the app to work.

By using Rooted you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, you can always reach us at rooted.app.help@gmail.com.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data needed to provide you with a working app experience:

  • Account information — email address and password (or social login token) when you create an account.
  • Habit data — the habits you create, your daily logs, and streak history. This data is yours and stored securely.
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version, used only for debugging and compatibility.
  • Usage data — anonymous interaction events (e.g. screens visited) to help us understand how the app is used.

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera access, or any sensitive personal data.

How We Use the Information

The data we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain the Rooted app and sync your data across devices.
  • Identify and fix bugs or technical issues.
  • Improve the app experience based on aggregate (anonymous) usage patterns.
  • Respond to your support requests.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for any purpose beyond operating and improving Rooted.

Notifications

Rooted may send you local push notifications to remind you to log your habits. These reminders are configured entirely by you inside the app and can be turned off at any time from your device's notification settings. We do not send marketing or promotional notifications.

Analytics

We may use lightweight, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how users interact with the app at an aggregate level. Any analytics data is anonymised before processing and is never tied to your personal identity.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in the cloud using Supabase, which is hosted on industry-standard infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

Third-Party Services

Rooted uses a small number of trusted third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and cloud storage.
  • Expo / EAS — app build and delivery platform.
  • RevenueCat (if premium features are enabled) — subscription management. RevenueCat's privacy policy applies to subscription data.

We do not integrate advertising SDKs or data brokers.

Children's Privacy

Rooted is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will notify you inside the app.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out:
